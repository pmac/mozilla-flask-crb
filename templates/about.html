About

The Open Web Device CRB (compliance review board) focuses on:

  • API compatibility
  • Minimum performance benchmarks

The Open Web Device CRB is an independent entity run by its Board of Directors. Members are a mix of partners covering geographically diverse markets.

Independent external labs will be authorized to perform test on devices to certify compliance with our performance and compliance specifications.
